Buying tokens on the Asteroid marketplace requires 2 transactions.

First, you must click the Reserve button on a specific listing and submit a nominal deposit fee. Once your reservation is confirmed, you'll have 100 blocks (~10 minutes) to click the Buy button and send the full purchase amount.

If you do not complete your purchase within 100 blocks, your reservation will be cancelled, and other traders will be able to initiate the buying process on that listing.

Important note The marketplace reservation process is designed to combat spam listings and potential "double-buy" transactions (learn more). However, it's still possible to lose your deposit in two ways: 1) if another user's deposit arrives seconds before yours; or 2) you successfully reserve a listing but do not finalize the purchase by sending the full amount within 100 blocks.
